Precise Temperature
When preparing a dish, especially when making baked goods, selecting the correct temperature is the most important. The Midea oven can regulate the temperature from 30 degrees to 250 degrees, which precisely adapts to the desired temperature for the product to be baked.
Universal Fit
The Midea MO96000MGB built-in electric oven is made of stainless steel, and its design fits well with any type of kitchen.
Convection Function
This convection function ensures an even distribution of heat to produce a uniform mass.
Practical Cleaning
The removable double-glass door is also practical for cleaning.
2-in-1 Oven
It is both an electric multifunction oven and a microwave oven.
17 Cooking Functions
It has 17 cooking functions, 4 of which are microwave functions.
